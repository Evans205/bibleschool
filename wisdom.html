Valuable Lessons and Life Skills from the Book of Proverbs

The Book of proverbs has 31 Chapters. A calendar has maximum of 31 days in a month. Therefore, the book of proverbs can be a Christian calendar. By reading the book of proverbs daily as part of your Bible study can really increase your practical like skills wisdom. The following verses where made during an inspiration Bible study group which decided to study the book of proverbs every day and each person to come up daily with a mediation verse. The following were the meditation verses of Prof Josphert Kimatu between May and June 2021. You can also start and develop another list of meditation nuggets as the Holy Spirit reveals His wisdom to you from the same book. It shall bless and enrich you greatly.

Words of the wise.

It is heavenly wisdom given by the resurrected Saviour as the Prince of peace after defeating Satan (Goliath) as David the despised King of Israel. The school of wisdom also has its school uniform. The Author clearly states the uniform one is to wear to enter into this school. This first qualification is called the fear of God. (verse 7). What to expect in the school of wisdom

Verse 3: To receive instruction of wisdom
Verse 4: To give discernment to the youth

Just imagine verse 7 says as a fool will despise wisdom and instruction. In science we say wisdom is the right application of knowledge. The path of the wise
Proverbs 2:6 For the LORD giveth wisdom: out of his mouth cometh knowledge and understanding. Verse analysis
The verse shows three things which come from One source. The three things: 1. Wisdom 2. Knowledge 3. Understanding The One source The LORD
Descriptions
The LORD is one of the Names of God. In Genesis chapter 1, He is the Creator. He is called Elohim. Which means The Mighty one. After Genesis 12 He reveals Himself as El Shaddai to Abraham. El Shaddai means the All sufficient One. Later in Exodus 3 He reveals Himself to Moses as Jehovah or Yehovah or Yahweh. The name means Redeemer. It is in this redemptive name that He gives wisdom in Proverbs 2:7. This means all truth is found only in understanding how God send His Son to redeem us. Actually, Jesus said He is the Truth (John 14:6).
The three things in the description
To understand the three things, we need to understand the path of life. When one wants something or has a problem he or she thinks hard. Then one comes up with an idea. If this idea works all the time at all places by all people is called truth. An accumulation of truths is called knowledge. If one fears God the he gets the ability to use the knowledge. The application of knowledge is called wisdom. The accurate timing to apply wisdom is called understanding. This is the path of life. Then, the Lord says from His mouth comes truth. John 17:17: says thy word is truth. In Luke 4:4 it says man shall not live by bread alone but by every word that comes out of the mouth of the LORD. Therefore, the path of life starts with God and ends with God. Is He not the Alpha and Omega? Hallelujah and glory to our Father God. Let's say a thankful Amen.
