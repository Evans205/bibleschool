How to get a marriage partner

Chapter One

The Value of Holiness

God Almighty the maker of heaven and earth is a holy God and He does not commit Himself to a person who is not holy. Hence, He deals with a person on the basis of how committed they are to be holy. For example, God did not just tell Abraham that He would bless him and make him a father of many nations without several conditions which would safeguard His holiness. There were conditions like, get out of your country, your relatives and your father’s house.

Genesis 12:1-3

Now the LORD had said unto Abram, Get thee out of thy country, and from thy kindred, and from thy father's house, unto a land that I will shew thee:
2 And I will make of thee a great nation, and I will bless thee, and make thy name great; and thou shalt be a blessing:
3 And I will bless them that bless thee, and curse him that curseth thee: and in thee shall all families of the earth be blessed.
The clear understanding of the holiness of God is very crucial if we desire to have the full revelation of His leading in sensitive matters like marriage partners. The Word of God clearly says that we should “follow holiness without which no man shall see the Lord”. This does not just mean one shall not go to heaven, but also includes that a man shall not see God in action in his or her life here on earth.

Hebrews 12:14

14 Follow peace with all men, and holiness, without which no man shall see the Lord:

What is holiness? Holiness is to be set apart for God’s use. This means that anything that has been separated for God’s use that thing is declared holy. If you declare that your house is God’s property, that house becomes holy. If some men build a house and then set it apart as God’s property, that building becomes holy. This setting a part of a thing or somebody for God’s use is called dedication. The Bible says that every dedicated thing is holy for the LORD. Other words can also be used like sanctification, consecration or even committed. God is a holy God and everything He touches becomes holy. For example when He appeared in a normal wilderness bush, He declared to Moses to remove His shoes because the place where He stood was holy.

Exodus 3:5

And he said, Draw not nigh hither: put off thy shoes from off thy feet, for the place whereon thou standest is holy ground.

There is a difference between holiness and Purity.

I tend to believe that purity is a higher level of holiness. This implies that a thing or a person that has been set apart for God is holy. But, can now be cleansed more from every form of filthiness and be declared pure. Purification is more practical than holiness. The Bible says we should be holy, but it also says we should be pure by perfecting holiness. Purity is tested holiness. It is tested purity that enters into God's presence. Then it understands God's will and it then qualifies to serve God.

1 Corinthians 7:1


Having therefore these promises, dearly beloved, let us cleanse ourselves from all filthiness of the flesh and spirit, perfecting holiness in the fear of God.

1 John 3:2-3

Beloved, now are we the sons of God, and it doth not yet appear what we shall be: but we know that, when he shall appear, we shall be like him; for we shall see him as he is. 3 And every man that hath this hope in him purifieth himself, even as he is pure.


Matthew 5:8

Blessed are the pure in heart: for they shall see God.
Hence, we can clearly see that our walk with a holy God demands that we be holy and we must keep on walking with the Holy Spirit in order for us to be pure; especially to be pure in heart.
One of the great desires of God is for us to have fellowship with Him eternally. This logically means that if God is holy, then we must desire to be holy as He is holy. Everything about God is holy and any human being who does not prioritize holiness and purity should forget any fellowship with the Holy God of Israel. Those who want to hear any message about marriage from this Holy God must decide to be holy. This is because even the Bible we are going to read is called Holy Bible, for a holy nation which is preparing to go to a holy heaven.

Revelation 4:8

And the four beasts had each of them six wings about him; and they were full of eyes within: and they rest not day and night, saying, Holy, holy, holy, Lord God Almighty, which was, and is, and is to come.

Psalms 20:6

Now know I that the LORD saveth his anointed; he will hear him from his holy heaven with the saving strength of his right hand.

1 Peter 1:15-16

But as he which hath called you is holy, so be ye holy in all manner of conversation; 16 Because it is written, Be ye holy; for I am holy.
Having settled the question of holiness we can now move on to show the way in which God leads His people to the right choices and what they are supposed to do.

Chapter Two

Identifying which kind of person you are

We come to God from various backgrounds. The background which one comes from is important in understanding how God will deal with the person. The Bible categorizes all the people on earth in three groups. The three groups are Jews, Gentiles and the Church of God.

1 Corinthians 10:31-33

Whether therefore ye eat, or drink, or whatsoever ye do, do all to the glory of God. 32 Give none offence, neither to the Jews, nor to the Gentiles, nor to the church of God: 33 Even as I please all men in all things, not seeking mine own profit, but the profit of many, that they may be saved.
The way God dealt with the Jews and Gentiles in old days can be read in the Old Testament. We shall hereby deal with the way God deals with those who enter into the Church of the living God either from the Jews or from the Gentle world. However, all who enter into the church are always brought into fellowship with the Lord Jesus Christ by the Holy Spirit.

The Rebecca type of people

According to my study of the Word of God I believe that there is a certain kind of people who have known about a holy God at early age or have known the way of holiness all through their lives. I compare these people with Rebecca who was brought up in the family which was connected to the knowledge of a holy God and was careful not to displease Him. This family could have heard how Abraham was called by a God called Elshaddai and how He told Abraham to walk before Him and be perfect (Genesis 17:1) and later God blessed him with all things.
Hence, there are several principles which God uses to guide such kind of people to have the right kind of marriage partners. I shall call this way the Rebecca way.

The Ruth type of people

The other category of people is the Ruth type of people. These are people who have come from a background which did not have any idea of a holy God. They lived and even worshipped other gods, did all kind of wickeness but they have repented and completely turned to seek a holy God for their lives. We can see how a Moabites called Ruth came to be in the lineage of Jesus Christ. Moabites are descendants of lot who came though the wine drinking idea and adultery incest of the daughters of Lot.

We can have two lines of interpretation from this passage. One is more literal and the other is concerning the Body of Christ the Church. I shall discuss the literal interpretation first.

Genesis 24:1

1 And Abraham was old, and well stricken in age: and the LORD had blessed Abraham in all things.

This shows that God has a right time at which human being can be able to discern things for their children. This is what is hereby called old age. Wisdom comes by age at times. Remember wisdom is the application of knowledge. Abraham must have accumulated much knowledge from God and from men.
