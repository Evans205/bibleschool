Tendo Junior Academy

Lukenya Bible School has started a Primary School called Tendo Junior Academy in the same premises with the Bible School so as to raise godly students. The school now has TSC Teachers and Students, lunch facilities, play grounds.
The pupils are both girls and Boys. We expect to get more students in July 2022. We are offering PPI, PP2 and Grade 1-6 . We now we have pupils have to grade 1.
