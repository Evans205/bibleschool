Five things we must doing now as Christians

As Christians we must not cease to meet and fellowship (Hebrews 10:25). This is because there are five things which we must be doing every day.


1. We must be building on our most holy faith (Praying and studying the Word). – Jude 20, Colossians 3:16
2. We must be fighting the good fight of faith (against flesh, world and the devil).- Ephesians 6:12, Ephesians 4:27
3. We must be breaking bread to remember the Lord’s death. 1 Corinthians 11:24-25
4. We must be serving the living God on earth. Colossians 3:23-24
5. We must be waiting for the glorious appearing of the Lord. (Watch and Praying). Titus 2:13.


The supporting verses:
Jude 20: But ye, beloved, building up yourselves on your most holy faith, praying in the Holy Ghost,

Ephesians 6: 11-12: Put on the whole armour of God, that ye may be able to stand against the wiles of the devil.

12 For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places.

Ephesians 4: 27: Neither give place to the devil.

1 Corinthians 11:23- 25: For I have received of the Lord that which also I delivered unto you, That the Lord Jesus the same night in which he was betrayed took bread:24 And when he had given thanks, he brake it, and said, Take, eat: this is my body, which is broken for you: this do in remembrance of me. 25 After the same manner also he took the cup, when he had supped, saying, This cup is the new testament in my blood: this do ye, as oft as ye drink it, in remembrance of me

Colossians 3:23: And whatsoever ye do, do it heartily, as to the Lord, and not unto men;24 Knowing that of the Lord ye shall receive the reward of the inheritance: for ye serve the Lord Christ.

Titus 2:13: Looking for that blessed hope, and the glorious appearing of the great God and our Saviour Jesus Christ;
