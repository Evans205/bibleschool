The glorious Keys of fasting

The Glorious Keys of Fasting
What is fasting?:

 Definition:
Fasting is voluntary abstinence from food for spiritual purposes.
1. It is not forced by an external person but it is a choice.
2. One chooses to say no to physical food so that one can feed on spiritual food.
3. It is triggered by a desire for God which is beyond the natural desires.

Are there people who fasted?

Yes…great men and great nations have been known to fast.

Example: Jesus, Moses, Daniel, Paul, Elijah, David etc were men who knew the power and benefits of fasting.
Some religions even require the followers to fast so as to strengthen beliefs.
Example: Christianity, Buddhism, Islam etc
Are there medical benefits for fasting?
Yes even doctors recommend that fasting is good for the body.
1.Fasting makes the body to rest.
2.Fasting helps the body to remove toxic substances.
3.Fasting helps the body to burn excess fat.
4.Fasting sharpens mental abilities

Types of fasting

1. Partial fasting

 In partial fast one can abstain from particular types of foods for some time. It can take a long time. For example. Daniel 10:3 I ate no pleasant bread, neither came flesh nor wine in my mouth, neither did I anoint myself at all, till three whole weeks were fulfilled.

2. Total fasting

 This means no water no food. It can only be to a maximum of three days. Or else you can damage brain cells due to much dehydration. After all kinds of fasting one has to take small soft foods and liquids gradually so that the body can adjust again. TOO much instant solid in breaking the fast is dangerous for the body.

Kinds of fasting
1. Individual fasting: one fasts alone for personal breakthroughs.
2. Church fasting: For the church to grow and be established
3. National fasting: For a whole country to return to God or seek an intervention.
4. Corporate fasting among several people like a family or factory.

The Length of fasting

Fasting can take duration between 1 day to 40 days. The main categories are;

1. 1 Day: Daniel 6:18 (It only enough for mostly individual spiritual survival only)
2. 3 days: Esther 4:16, Acts 9:9 (For those who seek deep understanding of the Word)
3. 7 days: I Samuel 31:13 (For those who want to know the depth of God’s Holiness)
4. 14 days: Acts 27:33-34 (For those who want the manifestation of spiritual gifts)
5. 21 days: Daniel 10:3-13 (For those who want more in deep visions)
6. 40 days: Exodus 24:18, I Kings 19:8, Matthew 4:2 (For established miracle power)
